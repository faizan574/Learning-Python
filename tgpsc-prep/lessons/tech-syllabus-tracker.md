# GATE Measuring Instruments — Syllabus Coverage Tracker

Technical daily-revision track at GATE depth. The Routine reads this file, picks the FIRST
unticked topic (one per day — a single deep dive, not five subjects), and ticks it after
covering it. Order is deliberate: fundamentals → instruments → applications. When all are
ticked, a second pass of mixed numerical-practice days begins (mark ✓✓).

- [ ] Basics of measurements: static characteristics (accuracy, precision, resolution, sensitivity, linearity, drift), dynamic characteristics, standards & calibration
- [ ] Error analysis I: types of errors (gross, systematic, random), absolute/relative/limiting errors, error propagation in sums, products, quotients
- [ ] Error analysis II: statistical analysis — mean, deviation, standard deviation, probable error, Gaussian distribution, combination of quantities
- [ ] Galvanometers: d'Arsonval (torque equation, damping, CDRX), ballistic galvanometer, flux meter
- [ ] PMMC instruments: construction, torque equation, swamping resistance, errors; extension of range — shunts, multipliers, Ayrton shunt (numericals)
- [ ] Moving Iron (MI) instruments: attraction & repulsion types, torque from energy principle, scale shape, AC/DC errors, hysteresis & frequency errors
- [ ] Electrodynamometer (EMMC) instruments: torque equation, use as ammeter/voltmeter, transfer instrument concept, errors
- [ ] Electrostatic, induction, thermal (thermocouple/hot-wire) & rectifier instruments: principles, true-RMS vs average-responding, form-factor errors
- [ ] Measurement of power I: dynamometer wattmeter — connections, pressure-coil/current-coil errors & compensation, LPF wattmeter modifications
- [ ] Measurement of power II: three-phase power — Blondel's theorem, two-wattmeter method (pf angle derivations, sign cases), reactive power measurement
- [ ] Measurement of energy: single-phase induction energy meter — driving/braking torques, lag adjustment, creeping, friction compensation, errors, testing & phantom loading (numericals)
- [ ] DC potentiometer: Crompton type, standardisation with standard cell, applications (calibration of ammeter/voltmeter/wattmeter, resistance measurement); AC polar & coordinate potentiometers
- [ ] Instrument transformers — CT: theory, equivalent circuit, phasor diagram, ratio & phase-angle error derivations, burden, effects of open secondary, testing
- [ ] Instrument transformers — PT: phasor diagram, errors, comparison with CT; numericals on ratio/phase errors
- [ ] DC bridges I: Wheatstone bridge — sensitivity, galvanometer deflection, limitations; Carey-Foster slide-wire
- [ ] DC bridges II: low resistance — Kelvin double bridge; high resistance — loss of charge method, megger, guard circuits
- [ ] AC bridges I: general balance equations; inductance bridges — Maxwell's inductance-capacitance, Hay's, Anderson (Q-factor ranges, derivations)
- [ ] AC bridges II: capacitance & frequency bridges — Schering (tan δ), De Sauty, Wien bridge; universal bridge; sources & detectors, Wagner earthing
- [ ] CRO: CRT construction, electrostatic deflection derivation, deflection sensitivity, time-base, synchronisation, measurement of voltage/frequency/phase, Lissajous patterns (numericals), probes (10:1), DSO basics
- [ ] DVM & digital instruments: ramp, dual-slope (derivation, noise rejection), successive approximation, resolution & quantisation, specifications (digits, counts), DMM architecture
- [ ] Q-meter, frequency & phase measurement, PMMC-based ohmmeters (series/shunt), earth-resistance measurement
- [ ] Grand revision day I: formula sheet + 25 mixed GATE PYQ-style numericals (instruments & power/energy)
- [ ] Grand revision day II: formula sheet + 25 mixed GATE PYQ-style numericals (bridges, potentiometers, CT/PT, CRO, DVM)
