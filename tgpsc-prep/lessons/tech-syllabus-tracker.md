# GATE Technical Revision — Syllabus Coverage Tracker

Three GATE (EE) subjects run **in parallel** in the 3 PM slot: **Measuring Instruments**,
**Electrical Machines**, and **Power Electronics**. Each daily lesson covers the FIRST unticked
topic from **each** of the three sections (one topic per subject per day), each with a deep
dive, formula sheet, solved examples and a test. The Routine ticks all three after covering
them. When a section is fully ticked, its slot switches to a deeper revision / numerical-practice
pass (mark ✓✓). Goal: complete all three subjects together.

Day-numbering note: `tech-day-N.md` is one lesson containing all three sections for that day.
